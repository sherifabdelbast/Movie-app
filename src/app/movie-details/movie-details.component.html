Overview

Set in the brutal and competitive landscape of the American mafia, "Mobland" follows the rise of undercover FBI agent Max Romano as he infiltrates one of the most dangerous crime families in the country. As Max gets deeper into his role, the line between his true identity and his criminal persona begins to blur, threatening both his mission and his life. The series explores themes of loyalty, betrayal, and the moral compromises made in the pursuit of justice.

Season 1

2024 | 10 Episodes

In the first season of Mobland, we follow FBI agent Max Romano as he assumes a new identity to infiltrate the powerful Vitale crime family. As he earns the trust of patriarch Vincent Vitale, Max finds himself increasingly entangled in the family's illegal operations and personal dynamics, especially when he develops feelings for Vincent's daughter, Elena. Meanwhile, a rival mob family threatens the delicate balance of power in the city.
